Web Development Trends 2024

By Jane Smith Published: March 14, 2024

Share this article

The web development landscape continues to evolve rapidly in 2024, bringing new technologies and methodologies to the forefront.

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)

PWAs continue to gain traction, offering app-like experiences through web browsers. They provide offline functionality, push notifications, and fast loading times.

WebAssembly

WebAssembly is revolutionizing web performance, enabling high-performance applications to run directly in the browser.

AI-Powered Development

Artificial Intelligence is increasingly being integrated into development workflows, from code completion to automated testing.
